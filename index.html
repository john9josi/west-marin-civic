About

West Marin Civic is built by and built for those of us on this side of the hill. We're building the digital infrastructure to keep West Marin safe, starting with local road closures and emergencies.

We don't have a profit incentive. We don't want to capitalize on crisis. We're just having fun and doing good.

Please donate to our coffee fund (read: pay for server costs).

And, if you want to go all out and max your contributions, tell a friend or a neighbor about us. The more of us using West Marin Civic, the better off we will all be.
