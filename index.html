What this is

West Marin Civic shows real-time status for what matters: road closures, power outages, fire alerts, and severe weather. One page, no login — always free.

How to read the status

🟢 Green — All clear. No active alerts.

🟠 Orange — Watch or warning. Conditions could turn. Stay aware.

🔴 Red — Active emergency. Something is happening right now. Tap for details.

Where the data comes from

We pull directly from official sources:

  • Road closures: 511.org
  • Power outages: PG&E
  • Weather and fire weather: National Weather Service
  • Active fires: WFIGS (federal fire data)

We monitor the feeds continuously. If one breaks, we say so at the top of the page and in the footer — we don't quietly show stale data.

Who we are
